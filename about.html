About Us

Eco‑Automation Corner is a community‑focused hub that demystifies sustainable building technology. We bring together facility managers, architects, engineers, and curious homeowners to explore how intelligent controls, sensor networks, and low‑impact design can make indoor environments healthier, more comfortable, and kinder to the planet.

Our Mission

• Translate cutting‑edge research into practical guidance.
• Showcase real‑world case studies of energy‑saving retrofits.
• Foster an open dialogue about the future of smart, low‑impact buildings.

Who We Are

The site is maintained by a small team of sustainability enthusiasts and tech hobbyists. We’re not a commercial vendor—we’re a learning space powered by curiosity and a desire to reduce the carbon footprint of the built environment.
